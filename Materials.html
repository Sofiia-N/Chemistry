Procedure


1. We poured a small amount of red cabbage juice into each test tube or glass container.

2. One by one, we added a small quantity of each test substance to the cabbage juice using a dropper.

3. We gently stirred or shook each mixture.

4. We observed the color change that occurred and recorded the results in our observation sheet.

5. We compared the final color with the pH scale to classify the substance as acidic, neutral, or basic, and estimated its pH value.

6. We repeated the process for all the substances listed.
