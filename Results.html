Conclusion

The experiment demonstrated that red cabbage juice is a reliable natural pH indicator. It clearly changed color depending on the acidity or basicity of the tested substances.
Our hypothesis was correct — acidic substances produced red/pink colors, neutral substances kept the juice purple, and basic substances shifted it
to green, blue, or yellow. This simple yet effective method could be used for testing pH in everyday life using natural materials.
